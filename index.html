Pronounced Ëˆgru-ia'

A wondrous berry, from a land far away

The Grewia Project exists to introduce the Western world to a berry that is as obscure as it is nutritious--while supporting farmers and merchants in Chad. Food and drinks made with Grewia are staples in both Chadian and Sudanese households, often used to combat ailments like stomach infections and anemia. Grewia juice's high vitamin content and distinctive, yet familiar tropical flavor have made it an attractive choice for health enthusiasts. The Grewia project has also fostered trade relationships that will make it easier to bring other Chadian staples to a number of global markets.
